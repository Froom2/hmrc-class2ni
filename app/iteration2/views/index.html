Class 2 National Insurance

You have to pay Class 2 National Insurance when in self-employment.

Use this service to:

  • view your periods of self-employment
  • change periods of self-employment if the dates we have are incorrect
  • understand the amount of Class 2 National Insurance you have to pay
  • recalculate payments if you change any self-employment dates

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Start

What you need to know

  • any gaps between dates may be because you claimed a benefit where you received credits or you stopped and re-started self-employment
  • you still have to pay Class 2 National Insurance during a temporary break in self-employment if you plan to continue with self-employed work

You can make changes outside of the relevant tax year.
