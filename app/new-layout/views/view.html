What is a temporary break in self-employment?

You still have to pay Class 2 NICs during a temporary break if you intend to continue being self-employed. This includes:

  • taking a holiday abroad or in the UK;
  • gaps between self-employed projects;
  • times you are not carrying out self-employed work, but still actively seeking it and advertising for it;
  • writers undertaking research;
  • on jury service.
