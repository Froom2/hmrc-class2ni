What you have to pay

£106.40
Tax year 6 April 2014 to 5 April 2015 (52 weeks)

Class 2 contributions are £2.75 a week for the tax year 2014 to 2015 and are paid as part of your Self-Assessment.

What you need to know

  • any gaps between dates may be because you claimed a benefit where you got Employment and Support Allowance (ESA), were working abroad or you stopped and re-started self-employment
  • you still have to pay Class 2 National Insurance during a temporary break in self-employment if you intend to continue with self-employment
  • if you choose to delete a period where you have to pay Class 2 National Insurance, it will be permanently deleted and can't be recovered

Changing dates or deleting a period where you have to pay may impact:

  • how many qualifying years you have for State Pension
  • the amount of State Pension you’re likely to get
  • certain benefits you are entitled to
