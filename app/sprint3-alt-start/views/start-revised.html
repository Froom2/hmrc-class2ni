What you have to pay

£106.40
Tax year 2014 to 2015

Class 2 contributions are £2.75 a week for the tax year 2014 to 2015 and are collected through your Self Assessment return.

Use this service to:

  • view the periods where you have to pay Class 2 National Insurance
  • change your dates if any are incorrect
  • understand how much Class 2 National Insurance you have to pay
  • recalculate how much you have to pay if you change or delete any periods
  • The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

What you need to know

  • any gaps between dates may be because you claimed Employment and Support Allowance (ESA), were working abroad or you stopped and re-started self-employment
  • you still have to pay Class 2 National Insurance during a temporary break in self-employment if you intend to continue with self-employment

Changing dates or deleting a period where you have to pay may impact:

  • how many qualifying years you have for State Pension
  • the amount of State Pension you’re likely to get
  • certain benefits you are entitled to
