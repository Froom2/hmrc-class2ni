Class 2 National Insurance

You can view, change and recalculate your periods of Class 2 National Insurance liability for the relevant tax year.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Start

What you need to know

  • Your Class 2 National Insurance weekly liability starts on a Sunday and ends on a Saturday, so the dates shown may differ from the standard tax year (06 April to 05 April)
  • Any gaps between your periods of liability may be because you claimed a benefit where you received credits or you stopped and re-started self-employment.

You still have to pay Class 2 National Insurance during a temporary break in self-employment if you plan to continue with self-employed work.

You can make changes outside of the relevant tax year.
