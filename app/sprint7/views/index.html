Information Gaps between periods are when you informed HMRC you weren't in or actively seeking self-employment.

Temporary breaks aren't shown, as you still have to pay Class 2 National Insurance during a temporary break, if you intend to continue being self-employed.

What's classed as a temporary break in self-employment?
  • times you're not carrying out self-employed work, but still actively seeking it and advertising for it
  • gaps between self-employed projects
  • taking holidays abroad or in the UK
  • writers undertaking research
  • jury service

If you think there's a period missing, you can add periods of self-employment by using form CWF1 on gov.uk.
